Hints

  • How to add and connect shapes

    Drag shapes from the menu. Activate the shape with a click. Pull out the arrow.

  • How to edit shapes

    The first click activates the shape, the second click opens the settings.

  • How to select multiple shapes

    On a PC, use the right mouse button.
    On phone: long press.

  • Selecting multiple shapes
    by holding Shift

  • Hotkeys

    Ctrl+C, Ctrl+V, Ctrl+X - copy, paste, cut
    Ctrl+Z, Ctrl+Y - undo, redo
    Del, Backspace - delete selected shapes
    Shift + mouse click on figure - select multiple shapes
